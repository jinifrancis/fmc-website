A community of faith
in the heart of West Koratty

Fathima Matha Church — stands as a beacon of faith for our vibrant Syro-Malabar Catholic community. Established in 1960 under the Ernakulam–Angamaly Archeparchy, our parish welcomes over 1,800 faithful from around 431 families in the Padinjare Koratty and Mambra areas.

Our Spiritual Heritage

Dedicated to Our Lady of Fatima, our patroness whose apparitions in Portugal inspire devotion worldwide, the church reflects Koratty's deep Marian tradition. Nearby, the historic St. Mary's Church (Koratty Muthy) from the 14th century enriches our local Christian roots.

Our church is more than a place of worship — it is a family hub. Active groups include catechism classes for children, youth ministry, choir, and charitable outreach. From vibrant feasts to family retreats, we build bonds in faith, service, and joy, always under Fathima Matha's protective gaze.

Farewell to Fr. Jomon Paliakkara

📅 February 22, 2026

After 5 years of dedicated service to our parish, Fr. Joseph (Jomon) Paliakkara will be transferring to St. Thomas Church, Kokkamangalam, Alapuzha. We extend our heartfelt gratitude for his spiritual guidance and pray for his continued ministry. A farewell gathering will be held on February 22nd, after the Sunday morning mass. His last day with us in our parish will be on February 28th.

Welcoming Fr. George (Varkey) Padayattil: We joyfully welcome Fr. George Padayattil as our new parish priest, who will join us on March 1st.
